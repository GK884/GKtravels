Places to Explore in Rajasthan


  • Jaipur


    • Jaipur is known as the Pink City of India Udaipur was the capital of Mewar Kingdom which is a city located in the state of Rajasthan. There are many exploring sites here namely, City palace, Lake Pichola, Saheliyon ki Bari, Jagmandir and Jagdish Temple to just name the major ones.

  • Udaipur


    • Renowned as the White City of Rajasthan, Udaipur is the most discussed! It is surrounded by artificial lakes and is known mostly for its royal residencies. It was the former capital city of Mewar witnessing tourists at the Lake Pichola flourished with peacock mosaics.


  • Jaisalmer

    • Jaisalmer is known as the golden city that is a significant tourist location in Rajasthan located in the north western state of Rajasthan, India. The golden sand dunes and all the castles that are located here in this place make it the golden city along with the enchanting lakes, opulent temples, and Havelis along with the exciting camel rides offering you an unforgettable experience.


  • Jodhpur

    • Popularly known as the Blue City, Jodhpur is located in the Thar Desert which has the 15th century-old Mehrangarh Fort. It is set upon a rocky base where numerous buildings are painted in the shade of blue from where the city derives its name.
